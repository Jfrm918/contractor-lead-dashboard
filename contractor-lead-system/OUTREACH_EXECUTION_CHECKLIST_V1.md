# Contractor Lead Conversion System — Outreach Execution Checklist V1

## Objective
Give Jason's wife a simple daily workflow for outreach.

---

## Daily Workflow
### 1. Pull target list
- work from the outreach sheet
- focus on best-fit trades first
- prioritize businesses likely buying leads

### 2. First call block
- make first call attempts
- log outcome immediately
- collect email when possible

### 3. Send follow-up email
- send cold email or resend requested info
- log email sent date

### 4. Second call block
- follow up with voicemail leads, email opens, and callback requests

### 5. Book next step
Goal is one of these:
- demo booked
- email collected
- callback time set

---

## End-of-Day Review
- how many companies called?
- how many owners reached?
- how many emails sent?
- how many demos booked?
- what objections came up most?

---

## Weekly Goal
The point is consistency, not perfect calls.

Track:
- calls made
- conversations started
- demos booked
- follow-ups needed

---

## Rule
Do not overcomplicate the process.
Keep it moving.
