# Contractor Lead Conversion System — Post-Demo Follow-Up Email V1

## Objective
Give Jason a clean follow-up email to send after a demo.

The goal is to:
- recap the value simply
- keep momentum
- make the next step easy
- move toward a pilot or onboarding conversation

---

# 1. Best Default Follow-Up Email
## Subject
- good talking today
- demo follow-up
- quick follow-up
- next steps

## Body
Hi [First Name],

Good talking with you.

As a quick recap, the system is designed to help you recover missed inbound leads, qualify them faster, and get better visibility into what happens after a call comes in.

For your team, the main value is making sure missed calls and slow follow-up don’t quietly waste leads you’re already paying for.

The pilot setup is simple:
- $500 setup
- $500/month

That includes the missed-call recovery flow, qualification setup, alerts, dashboard access, and monthly reporting.

If you want to move forward, the next step is just a short intake so we can configure the number, service area, lead rules, and alert flow.

If you want, I can send that over.

— [Name]

# 5. Best CTA
Best CTA after demo:
**“If you want, I can send over the intake and next steps.”**

Why:
- low pressure
- moves toward action
- easy yes/no decision

---

# 6. Best Practice
Do not over-explain after the demo.

The follow-up email should:
- remind them what the system does
- remind them of the pricing
- make the next step easy

That’s it.
