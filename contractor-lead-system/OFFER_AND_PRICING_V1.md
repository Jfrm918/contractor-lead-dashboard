# Contractor Lead Conversion System — Offer and Pricing V1

## Objective
Define the first sellable version of the offer.

This document answers:
- what exactly we are selling
- who we are selling it to
- how to explain it simply
- what we deliver
- how much we charge
- how to structure the first pilot deals

---

# 1. Core Offer
## What we sell
We help contractors convert more of the leads they already pay for by instantly following up on missed calls, qualifying prospects, and helping book more estimates.

This is a **lead conversion service**, not a generic AI service.

---

# 2. Best Positioning
## Do NOT say
- AI automation agency
- chatbot service
- lead gen magic
- full marketing replacement

## Say
- missed-call recovery
- faster speed-to-lead
- lead qualification
- estimate booking support
- better conversion from paid leads

This makes the offer easier to understand and easier to buy.

---

# 3. Who It Is For
## Best fit customers
Home-service / contractor businesses that:
- already buy leads
- already run Google Ads or LSAs
- rely on phone calls
- miss calls sometimes
- do not respond instantly every time
- would benefit from recovering expensive inbound leads

## Best starting niches
- HVAC
- plumbing
- roofing
- electrical
- insulation
- garage doors

---

# 4. Problem We Solve
These businesses already spend money to make the phone ring.

But they lose revenue when:
- calls are missed
- after-hours leads go cold
- response is slow
- junk leads waste office time
- good leads never get pushed toward booking

So the offer is not about “getting more traffic.”
It is about **converting more of the leads they already have**.

---

# 5. Core Promise
## Main promise
Recover missed opportunities and turn more inbound leads into booked estimates.

## What we can honestly promise
- faster follow-up
- missed-call recovery
- better lead visibility
- cleaner qualification
- faster routing of hot leads

## What we should NOT promise
- guaranteed revenue
- guaranteed close rates
- guaranteed booked jobs
- magic AI claims

---

# 6. Deliverables
## V1 deliverables
For each client, we deliver:
- tracked number setup
- call forwarding setup
- missed-call text-back system
- basic qualification flow
- owner / office alerts
- lead dashboard access
- monthly scorecard
- ongoing optimization and support

That is enough for a clear recurring offer.

---

# 7. Offer Name Ideas
Use whichever feels strongest later, but the offer itself matters more than the name.

Possible naming directions:
- Lead Recovery System for Contractors
- Contractor Lead Conversion System
- Missed Call Recovery + Booking System
- Paid Lead Conversion System

Best plain-English label for now:
**Contractor Lead Conversion System**

---

# 8. Pricing Recommendation
## Recommended starting price
### Setup fee
**$500–$1,000 setup**

### Monthly fee
**$500–$1,000/month**

This is the right starting range for early sales.

---

# 9. Best Starting Price Point
## My recommendation
Start with:
- **$500 setup**
- **$500/month**

Why:
- easier first sale
- easier pilot close
- low friction for proof stage
- still high enough to matter

Once you have proof:
move toward:
- $1,000 setup
- $750–$1,500/month

---

# 10. Pilot Offer Structure
## Best pilot structure
For first few clients:
- discounted setup
- discounted monthly retainer
- shorter commitment window if needed
- clear expectation that price increases after proof stage

## Recommended pilot version
**Pilot:**
- $500 setup
- $500/month
- first 60–90 days as proof period

After proof:
- move to normal pricing if results and fit are good

---

# 11. What the Client Is Actually Paying For
Each month they are paying for:
- missed-call recovery coverage
- automated first response
- qualification flow maintenance
- alerts and routing
- dashboard visibility
- scorecard reporting
- optimization and support

So this is not “software rental only.”
It is a managed conversion service.

---

# 12. Why This Offer Is Sellable
It ties to real money because:
- they already spend money on ads/leads
- missed calls are expensive
- speed-to-lead matters
- owners understand phone-call leakage immediately

This is easier to sell than abstract AI services.

---

# 13. Short Sales Pitch
## One-liner
We help contractors convert more of the leads they already pay for by instantly following up on missed calls, qualifying prospects, and helping book more estimates.

## Slightly longer version
If your business is already paying for Google leads, LSAs, or inbound calls, missed calls and slow follow-up are costing you money. We put a lead recovery and qualification system on top of your existing flow so more of those leads get contacted, qualified, and pushed toward booking.

---

# 14. What Makes This Easy to Buy
The offer should be framed as:
- easy to add
- easy to remove
- no major system replacement
- low risk
- fast to launch

That reduces resistance.

---

# 15. Commitment Recommendation
## Best starting approach
Do not force long contracts first.

Use one of these:
- month-to-month after setup
- or 90-day pilot

My recommendation:
**90-day pilot framing, billed monthly**

Why:
- enough time to see data
- not scary to the buyer
- still gives you runway

---

# 16. Risk Reversal Framing
Do not over-promise money-back gimmicks yet.

Safer framing:
- quick setup
- easy cancellation
- clear monthly visibility
- no deep disruption to current systems

That lowers buying friction without stupid guarantees.

---

# 17. Target Result for First 3 Clients
The goal with early clients is not max pricing.
The goal is:
- proof
- screenshots
- better numbers
- testimonials
- a repeatable process

That is what makes later pricing stronger.
