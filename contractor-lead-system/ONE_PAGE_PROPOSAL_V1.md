# Contractor Lead Conversion System
## Recover missed opportunities. Convert more inbound leads.

If your business is already paying for leads or making the phone ring through Google Ads, LSAs, referrals, or inbound traffic, missed calls and slow follow-up can quietly waste a lot of that spend.

The Contractor Lead Conversion System helps solve that by following up on missed calls fast, qualifying leads, flagging hot opportunities, and giving your team better visibility into what happens after a lead comes in.

---

## What this helps with
- missed inbound calls
- after-hours lead leakage
- slow speed-to-lead

## What’s included
- tracked number setup

- lead qualification flow

- client dashboard access

- ongoing optimization and support

---

## How it works
1. A lead calls in.
2. If the call is missed, the system follows up automatically by text.
3. The lead is qualified through a simple response flow.
4. Hot leads or callback requests get flagged fast.
5. The business gets better visibility into leads, activity, and booking opportunities.
