# Contractor Lead Conversion System — Cold Email Sequence V1

## Objective
Give Jason's wife a simple cold email sequence for contractor outreach.

The goal is:
- get opens
- get replies
- get demo interest
- not sound like spam

---

# 1. Core Rules
Use:
- short emails
- plain language
- low-friction CTA
- direct subject lines

Do not use:
- giant paragraphs
- fake personalization
- hypey AI language
- too many links
- marketing-agency buzzwords

---

# 2. Target
Best fit:
- contractors already buying leads
- businesses relying on inbound calls
- businesses where missed calls likely cost money

Best niches:
- HVAC
- plumbing
- roofing
- electrical
- insulation
- garage doors

---

# 3. Email 1 — Initial Outreach
## Subject line options
- quick question
- missed calls
- inbound leads
- quick idea for [Company Name]
- are missed calls costing you?

## Body
Hi [First Name],

Quick question — if a call gets missed at [Company Name], what usually happens next?

We help contractors recover missed inbound leads and follow up faster when calls get missed or come in after hours.

The goal is simple: convert more of the leads you already pay for into booked estimates.

Worth a quick look?

— [Name]

---

# 4. Email 2 — Follow-Up
Send 2–4 days later.

## Subject
- following up
- quick follow-up
- missed lead follow-up

## Body
Hi [First Name],

Following up here.

A lot of contractors are paying for Google leads or inbound calls but still lose opportunities when calls get missed or follow-up is slow.

We built a system that helps recover those missed leads, qualify them, and flag hot ones faster.

If you want, I can send over a quick overview.

— [Name]

---

# 5. Email 3 — Soft Pain Angle
Send a few days later.

## Subject
- one more question
- quick thought
- last note

## Body
Hi [First Name],

One quick question before I close this out:

Are you fully confident no paid leads are slipping through the cracks from missed calls or after-hours follow-up delays?

That’s the problem we help fix.

If it’s worth a quick look, I can send over a short demo.

— [Name]

---

# 6. Email 4 — Final Touch
## Subject
- close this out?
- should I close the loop?

## Body
Hi [First Name],

I’ll close the loop after this.

We help contractors convert more of the leads they already pay for by instantly following up on missed calls, qualifying prospects, and helping book more estimates.

If you ever want to see it, I can send the demo.

— [Name]

---

# 7. Best CTA
Best simple CTA:
- Worth a quick look?
- Want me to send the demo?
- Open to a quick 10-minute walkthrough?

Best recommendation:
**Want me to send the demo?**

Low friction wins.

---

# 8. Personalization Guidance
Use light personalization only:
- company name
- owner name
- city if relevant
- niche/trade mention

Do not write fake custom intros.

---

# 9. Offer Language to Reuse
Use this exact line often:

We help contractors convert more of the leads they already pay for by instantly following up on missed calls, qualifying prospects, and helping book more estimates.
