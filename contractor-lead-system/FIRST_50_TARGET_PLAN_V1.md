# Contractor Lead Conversion System — First 50 Target Plan V1

## Objective
Define the first 50 prospects to go after so outreach stays focused.

---

## Best starting mix
- 15 HVAC
- 10 plumbing
- 10 roofing
- 5 electrical
- 5 insulation
- 5 garage door

Why:
- strong phone dependence
- meaningful lead value
- likely to feel missed-call pain

---

## Geography recommendation
Start tight.

### First pass
- Tulsa
- Broken Arrow
- Owasso
- Bixby
- Jenks
- nearby service-area cities

Why:
- easier local credibility
- easier demo conversations
- easier pilot support early

---

## Selection filters
Prefer businesses that:
- clearly advertise online
- likely run Google Ads or LSAs
- have visible call-first websites
- are established enough to pay
- are not huge enterprises

Avoid first:
- tiny one-man shops with no spend
- giant companies with layers of approvals
- businesses with weak contact info

---

## Goal of first 50
Not to close all 50.
Goal:
- learn objections
- see which niche responds best
- book first demos
- get first pilot

---

## Suggested workflow
1. build list of 50
2. call first
3. email follow-up same day when possible
4. log every outcome
5. review weekly for pattern recognition
