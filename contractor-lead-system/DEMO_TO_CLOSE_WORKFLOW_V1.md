# Contractor Lead Conversion System — Demo to Close Workflow V1

## Objective
Define the simple step-by-step path from first demo to signed pilot.

---

## 1. Demo happens
Goal:
- show the problem
- show the dashboard
- show how easy it is to add
- give price simply

Desired outcome:
- interest
- pilot discussion
- permission to send next steps

---

## 2. Same-day follow-up
Send:
- post-demo follow-up email
- one-page proposal

CTA:
- “If you want, I can send over the intake and next steps.”

---

## 3. If interested
Move to:
- pilot confirmation
- intake form
- setup timeline

What to confirm:
- they want to test it
- who approves it
- best email and mobile
- who handles leads internally

---

## 4. If they stall
Follow-up with:
- short reminder
- pain-based question
- clear next step

Example:
- “Wanted to follow up. If missed calls and slow follow-up are still something you want to tighten up, I can send over the pilot setup steps.”

---

## 5. Close point
A deal is effectively closed when they say yes to:
- pilot price
- intake
- setup process

Do not overcomplicate this.

---

## 6. Post-close handoff
Immediately send:
- intake form
- onboarding expectations
- launch timeline

---

## 7. Best practice
The sales process should feel:
- simple
- clear
- low-friction
- not pushy

The goal is to move from:
- interest
- to demo
- to pilot
- to onboarding
