# Contractor Lead Conversion System — Pilot Agreement Terms V1

## Objective
Provide simple plain-English pilot terms for early clients.

---

## Pilot Offer
- Setup fee: **$500**
- Monthly fee: **$500/month**
- Pilot term: **90 days**

---

## Included
- tracked number setup
- missed-call text-back flow
- lead qualification flow
- hot lead alerts
- dashboard access
- monthly scorecard
- basic optimization/support during the pilot

---

## Client responsibilities
Client agrees to provide:
- intake information
- correct routing/contact details
- service area and service rules
- alert recipient details
- timely notice of changes to hours, services, or routing

---

## What success means
The pilot is intended to help the client:
- recover missed inbound leads
- improve follow-up speed
- improve lead visibility
- improve handling of inbound opportunities

No guarantee of revenue or booked jobs is made.

---

## Payment
- setup fee due before implementation
- monthly billing begins at launch or agreed start date

---

## Cancellation
- either side can end after pilot term
- client can cancel with written notice before next billing cycle
- tracked number/workflow access can be removed at cancellation

---

## Notes
This is a simple pilot agreement summary, not formal legal advice.
