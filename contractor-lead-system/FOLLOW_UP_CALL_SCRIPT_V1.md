# Contractor Lead Conversion System — Follow-Up Call Script V1

## Objective
Give Jason's wife a simple script for follow-up calls after an email, voicemail, or first conversation.

---

# 1. Follow-Up After Email
"Hey, this is [Name]. I sent you a quick note about helping contractors recover missed inbound leads and follow up faster when calls get missed. I just wanted to follow up and see if that’s something worth a quick look on your end."

---

# 2. Follow-Up After Voicemail
"Hey, this is [Name] following up from my voicemail. We help contractors recover missed inbound leads and make sure after-hours or missed calls don’t just disappear without follow-up. I wanted to see if that’s something worth a quick conversation."

---

# 3. Follow-Up After Initial Interest
"Hey, this is [Name]. We talked briefly about the missed-call recovery system for contractors. I wanted to follow back up and see if you’d be open to a quick walkthrough this week."

---

# 4. Best CTA
"Would you be open to a quick 10-minute walkthrough so you can see what it looks like?"

---

# 5. If They Say “Send It Again”
"Absolutely. I’ll resend it now. What’s the best email for you?"

---

# 6. If They Say “Not Right Now”
"No problem. Is that more of a timing thing, or is it just not something you’re looking at right now?"

---

# 7. Best Practice
The follow-up call is about momentum.
Do not re-pitch from scratch unless needed.
